FAQ

When do games start?

As soon as all invitations are sold, all users will be notified of this in all means of communication with the player and in private chat, also on our website.

What games will be and when will the player know which game will be?

Our games are not a random lottery and everything depends only on you! Defeat all opponents or Die. The best player wins the top prize. The name of the game, the rules of the game, the participant receives 24 hours before its start.

What does it mean to be deleted?

The removed player's invite token will be burned and you cannot participate in the next game.

How many players are kicked out in each game?

In each game, a different predetermined number of players fly out, but it all depends only on your strengths, and not on the random process.

How many players will share the prize?

The top prize will go to the best player who wins all five games of his opponents. The consolation prize will be shared by the last 4 players who reached the final. If I buy a player on the secondary market, will I still be able to claim a prize even if I have not played in previous games? Yes, any player available for sale in the secondary market has survived all previous games and still has a chance to survive the remaining games, regardless of who owns.

How can I participate in the games?

You can still buy one or more tokens at rarible.com

How can I be notified about new games?

All games will be announced on the games page of the site, Discord, Twitter, your contacts and in the Private chat.

What if I am at work or asleep when the game starts?

All games will be announced 24 hours in advance, so everyone will have the opportunity to find out and prepare in advance. The games are designed to provide an equal chance for all players, regardless of their time zone.

What if I miss the game?

Each game will last for 70 hours, enough time for anyone to participate.

What happens if I miss the game?

Your invitation will automatically expire and you cannot participate in the next game. Will I get something to replace my invitation card NFT if it is removed as a result of a loss? Yes, eliminated players can qualify for an NFT Invitation for another game in future games.
